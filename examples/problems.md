# Example Carpentry Problems

## Centering Items

### Problem 1: Recessed Lights
**Question:** How do I center 4 recessed lights in a 73-inch wide room?

**Answer:**
```
To center 4 light(s) in a 73.0" space:

Spacing (center to center): 14.625" (14 5/8")
Edge to first: 7.3125" (7 5/16")

Mark positions from left edge:
  Light 1: 7 5/16" on center
  Light 2: 1' 7 15/16" on center
  Light 3: 2' 6 9/16" on center
  Light 4: 3' 5 3/16" on center
```

### Problem 2: Outlet Spacing
**Question:** I need to space 5 outlets evenly in a 30-foot wall

**Answer:**
```
To center 5 item(s) in a 360.0" space:

Spacing (center to center): 60.0" (5' 0")
Edge to first: 30.0" (2' 6")

Mark positions from left edge:
  Item 1: 2' 6" on center
  Item 2: 5' 0" on center
  Item 3: 7' 6" on center
  Item 4: 10' 0" on center
  Item 5: 12' 6" on center
```

### Problem 3: Fixed Width Items
**Question:** How do I center 3 cabinets that are 18 inches wide in a 72-inch space?

**Answer:**
```
To center 3 item(s) in a 72.0" space:

Item width: 18.0"
Gap between items: 4.5" (4 1/2")
Edge to first item: 9.0" (9")

Measure from left edge:
  Item 1: 9" on center
  Item 2: 1' 7 1/2" on center
  Item 3: 2' 6" on center
```

## Stairs

### Problem 1: Deck Stairs
**Question:** Build stairs for a 10-foot deck height

**Answer:**
```
Stair Calculation for 10' 0" total rise:

Number of steps: 17
Rise per step: 7 1/16"
Run per step: 11"
Total run: 15' 7"
Stringer length: 18' 5 13/16"
Angle: 32.7° (4:12)
```

### Problem 2: Interior Stairs
**Question:** I need stairs for an 8-foot rise with 7-inch risers

**Answer:**
```
Stair Calculation for 8' 0" total rise:

Number of steps: 14
Rise per step: 6 6/7" (use 6 7/8")
Run per step: 11"
Total run: 12' 10"
Stringer length: 15' 2 3/16"
Angle: 31.8° (3.8:12)
```

## Concrete / Cubic Yards

### Problem 1: Patio Slab
**Question:** How many cubic yards of concrete for a 12x12 slab at 4 inches deep?

**Answer:**
```
Volume Calculation for 12.0' x 12.0' at 4.0" deep:

Cubic feet: 48.0
Cubic yards: 1.78

Concrete bags needed:
  80 lb bags: ~80
  60 lb bags: ~107
```

### Problem 2: Deck Footings
**Question:** How much concrete for 6 deck footings that are 18 inches diameter and 24 inches deep?

**Answer:**
```
Volume Calculation:

Each footing:
- Radius: 9"
- Height: 24"
- Volume: 0.87 cubic feet

Total for 6 footings:
- 5.24 cubic feet
- 0.19 cubic yards
- ~9 bags (80lb) or ~12 bags (60lb)
```

## Common Framing

### Stud Spacing
- Standard spacing: 16" on center
- Exterior walls in some areas: 24" on center
- Header spans: use span tables

### Standard Heights
- Wall height: 8', 9', 10'
- Door height: 6'8" (80")
- Window height: varies, but sills often at 36" AFF

### Plywood
- Standard sheet: 4' x 8'
- Subfloor: 3/4" tongue & groove
- Sheathing: 1/2" or 5/8"
